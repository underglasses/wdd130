About Us - Upstream

Our rafting experiences combine excitement, teamwork, and safety, making them perfect for both beginners and seasoned adventurers. Whether you’re chasing adrenaline or simply want to enjoy the outdoors, we’ll guide you through unforgettable journeys on the water.

History

UpStream began in 1999 with a small team of outdoor enthusiasts who loved exploring rivers. What started as weekend trips with friends quickly grew into a professional guiding service known for safety, fun, and unforgettable adventures.

Over the years, we have expanded to include multiple river routes, trained expert guides, and welcomed thousands of adventurers from around the world. Our passion for the outdoors and commitment to our guests has kept us paddling strong for more than two decades.
